Game Name: Flappy Professors

Description:
Game inspired by one of the most popular games in 2014, Flappy Bird. 
Mechanics are simple and very much like the original... but with our own twist. Pressing 'W' to control the "bird" character.
Dodge 10 obstacle pipes using to initiate a professor boss battle. When in battle, there will be no pipes dodging.
During boss battle, player will have to control character with 'W' and 'S' and shoot with 'Space'.
